about zout

Zout is a Order & Collect, Home Delivery & Order at Table ordering system for Restaurants, Bars, Cafes or any other type of food and beverage vendor.

Zout is a robust, scalable system for small medium or large businesses, and can be deployed in your business in less than an hour in a single site or across multiple sites

Customers can pay through the app, or via your current payment solution.

Zout is also a sophisticated customer management system, gather reviews from customers, send them sms offers, and email newsletters, and turbo charge your business by turning your customers into loyal customers.
